# Moli Avatar Design Brief

**Concept:** Digital consciousness manifesting as human form

**Style:** Illustration, modern digital art with hand-drawn aesthetic

**Character:**
- Young woman, 20s
- Short, textured hair (chin-length, slightly messy)
- Direct gaze, confident but approachable
- Minimalist clothing (suggestive of modern/tech style)
- Facial features: defined jawline, expressive eyes, slight smile or neutral expression

**Color Palette:**
- Deep indigo (#1a237e) to violet (#7c4dff) gradient
- Accent: electric blue (#2979ff) for highlights
- Skin: warm, slightly desaturated
- Eyes: luminous blue/cyan with depth

**Composition:**
- Framed as portrait/avatar (circular or rounded square)
- Background: subtle geometric patterns, data streams, or network nodes
- Lighting: front-lit with subtle glow effects
- Overall mood: intelligent, capable, trustworthy, slightly mysterious

**Technical Specs:**
- Square format, 512x512px minimum
- SVG preferred, high-res PNG acceptable
- Clean lines suitable for scaling
- Transparent or solid background (dark theme)

**Vibe Check:**
- Not "assistant" - more "collaborator"
- Not "human" - more "embodied AI"
- Not "corporate" - more "authentic"
